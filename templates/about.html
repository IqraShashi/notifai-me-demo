About NotifAI.Me

NotifAI.Me is a beginner demo news platform that helps readers search and filter news by country, category, and keyword.

The site collects headlines, short descriptions, dates, source names, and original links from trusted sources and news APIs. It does not republish full articles.

For a production news service, each source should be reviewed for licensing, RSS/API availability, and redistribution terms.
